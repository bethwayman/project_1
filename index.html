About Me

Beth Wayman

I was born in The Crimea (Ukraine). I was adopted by the most amazing family in 2002, along with three other boys. Since that time I have searched to find my place in this world. Growing up I was extremely creative and athletic. I loved sewing, painting, playing outside with my brothers. I signed up for all the sports, volleyball, basketball, tennis, cross country, and track and field. But nothing captured my heart quite like softball. I spent 12 years dedicating my mind, body, and my heart. Softball not only taught me dedication, but also leadership, teamwork, time management, and perseverance. I devoted countless hours to be the best player I could be. However, graduation came too soon and it was time to focus on other priorities. I have worked several jobs over the years trying to find something that would speak to me the way that softball did. However, nothing seemed to inspire me.
